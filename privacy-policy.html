Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026  ·  Velora  ·  Federal Republic of Nigeria

Welcome to Velora. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we handle it. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and what rights you have.

By registering on our platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

Who we are: Velora is a US fintech brand that empowers members to earn income through AI content creation, Commercial Consortia opportunities, and affiliate marketing. Our platform is accessible at velora.vercel.app and via our Android app on Google Play. For privacy-related questions, contact us at streamafrica77@gmail.com.
A note about our Android app: The Velora Android app does not independently collect, store, or process any personal data. All data collection takes place exclusively on our website when users choose to register. The app serves as a companion to the platform — the data practices described in this policy apply to website registration only.

1

What Data We Collect

When you register or use the Velora platform, we collect the following information:

  • Full Name — to identify your account
  • Phone Number — for account communication and verification
  • Email Address — for account creation, login, and updates
  • Payment Information — processed securely through Korapay; we do not store card details directly
  • Username — your chosen display name on the platform

We only collect data that is necessary to provide you with our services. We do not collect unnecessary personal information.

2

How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To register and create your Velora account
  • To process your one-time membership activation payment
  • To allocate your membership benefits and income streams
  • To send you important updates about your account or our platform
  • To provide customer support when you contact us
  • To comply with legal obligations where required

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

3

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party — ever.

The only situation where your data may be shared with a third party is with our payment processor, Korapay, solely for the purpose of completing your membership activation payment. Korapay operates under its own privacy policy and complies with Nigerian financial regulations.

We may also disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request from a Nigerian government authority.

In plain terms: Your data stays with us and our payment processor. Nobody else sees it, and we will never sell it.
4

Data Retention

We keep your personal data for as long as your Velora membership is active.

If you close your account or request deletion, we will retain your data for up to 90 days after account closure, after which it will be permanently deleted from our systems. This period allows us to resolve any outstanding issues or payment queries.

Payment transaction records may be kept for longer periods where required by Nigerian financial regulations.

5

Your Rights

As a Velora user, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction — ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion — request that we delete your personal data
  • Right to Object — object to how we process your data

To exercise any of these rights, email streamafrica77@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Request". We will respond within 14 business days.

To delete your account and data: Email streamafrica77@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account" and include the email linked to your Velora account. Your data will be removed within 90 days.
6

Children's Privacy

Velora is strictly intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. Our platform involves financial transactions, which are not appropriate for minors.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a minor has registered, we will immediately delete their account and all associated data.

If you believe a minor has registered on Velora, please contact us immediately at streamafrica77@gmail.com.

7

Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our platform is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • User data is stored securely in Google Firebase, which uses industry-standard encryption at rest
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Korapay — we never store your card or bank details on our servers
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorised personnel only

While we take every reasonable measure to protect your data, no system is 100% immune to security risks. Please use a strong, unique password and keep your login credentials private.

8

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, technology, or legal requirements.

When we make significant changes, we will notify you by email to the address linked to your account. The updated policy will also be published on this page with a new "Last updated" date at the top.

Your continued use of Velora after any update means you accept the revised policy.

9

Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and shall be interpreted in accordance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, including the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and applicable data protection regulations.

Any disputes arising from this policy shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Nigerian courts.
